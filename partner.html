For Institutions & Companies

Let's collaborate to create impactful solutions.

Organizations (whether companies, nonprofits, or startups) can support our mission by contributing real, low-risk tech projects for students to work on during our 12-week cohort.

Projects should be practical, non-critical to core operations, and ideally something you'd love to automate or improve but haven’t had bandwidth to tackle.

You can optionally provide a tech lead to guide the student team, or let us run the project independently through our internal mentorship and management structure. Projects can be assigned to one team or opened up to multiple teams as a challenge with varied solutions.

---

In return, you gain early access to a pool of motivated, emerging talent while receiving working prototypes or early builds tailored to your problem statement.You’ll also increase visibility as a community-focused innovator and potential future partner.

This is a zero-cost, zero-risk opportunity during our pilot (Fall 2025) run, no funding required. If the collaboration proves valuable, we’re open to formalizing longer-term partnerships in future cohorts.
